MEREDITH + BRIAN

5.12.2018 | Charlottesville, VA

Meredith Pearson and Brian Hulette are getting married on May 12, 2018, in Charlottesville, VA

We met in December 2012 at a company Christmas party, thanks to a mutual friend (shout out to Rachel!), and hit it off immediately. Brian made the trip to Richmond a few days later for our first date, then Meredith came back up to Northern Virginia for a New Year's party, and not long after that our relationship was official.

After countless more drives up and down both I-95 and US-29, one trip to italy, and a couple moves to Charlottesville, we're finally getting married, and we can't wait to celebrate with all of you!
